2020 award-winning stationeries

BRIDE/GROOM Glass Pen

"If only glass pen could be a practical writing tool!" —— From this idea, the designer has the glass pen with reassuring thickness and small length, which is easy to carry.

This glass pen is small and portable, and its ink bottle has just been filled with enough ink for one time. Dip in ink and you can write smoothly.

As the ink bottle has a small capacity and can be carried around at the same time, when you use it, you can quickly clean it by shaking the glass pen gently in the water.

Drawable Globe

This is a globe that can be seen, touched and written. The designer said that this design is to bring the globe more freedom.

This globe can be viewed easily from the north pole or the south pole. However, the globe can't roll at will, but swings and stands like a protrusion in a specified place, which is also careful designed.

In addition, you can use it freely. You can use colored pencils to smear the countries you want to go to, and you can also write and paint on them.

Stickers of Traditional Japanses Color

Japanese traditional color series stickers, with mild materials, show various traditional colors representing Japanese traditions.

The stickers are semilucent, and each color has a different name. By stacking the stickers one over another, you can also present new colors by yourself.
